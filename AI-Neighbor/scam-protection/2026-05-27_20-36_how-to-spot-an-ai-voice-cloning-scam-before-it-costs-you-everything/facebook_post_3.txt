HOOK:
If someone calls you in a panic and tells you not to tell anyone else in the family — hang up immediately.
BODY:
That instruction — "don't tell anyone" — is one of the clearest warning signs of an AI voice cloning scam. Real emergencies do not come with secrecy requirements. Scammers use urgency and isolation to stop you from thinking clearly. Knowing this one signal alone could be the difference between protecting your savings and losing them.
