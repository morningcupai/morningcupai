MAIN TIP (40 seconds):
Here is one of the most practical things you can do with AI right now. The night before your next doctor's appointment, open ChatGPT or Claude — both are completely free — and type in a little about your situation. Your age, your medications, what you are going in for, and anything else that has been on your mind health-wise. Then ask it to help you put together a list of questions to bring to your appointment.
What comes back is genuinely useful. Questions about side effects you never thought to ask about. Questions about alternatives or next steps. Questions that help you feel like a real partner in your own healthcare instead of someone just nodding along for fifteen minutes.
Print the list or keep it on your phone. Hand it to your doctor at the start of the appointment. Most doctors love a prepared patient. And you will walk out of that office feeling like you actually got what you came for.
