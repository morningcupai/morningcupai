HOOK:
A prepared patient gets better care. Here is the easiest way to become one.
BODY:
Before your next doctor's appointment, open ChatGPT or Claude and ask it to help you build a question list based on your medications, your concerns, and your reason for visiting. Print it out or keep it on your phone. Hand it to your doctor at the start of the appointment. It takes ten minutes and changes everything about how that visit goes.
