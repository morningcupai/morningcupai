You walk out of the doctor's office and immediately remember the three things you forgot to ask. Sound familiar?

It happens to almost everyone. But there is a simple fix. The night before your next appointment, spend ten minutes with a free AI tool like ChatGPT or Claude putting together your question list. Tell it your medications, your concerns, and what you are going in for. It will help you think of questions you might never have thought to ask on your own.
